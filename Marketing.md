##### Marketing

Welcome to the Xubuntu Marketing team wiki. This page is a starting
point for all the marketing projects ongoing or planned in Xubuntu. To
get involved, add your ideas to the Future ideas and get in touch with
us at #xubuntu-devel on Freenode.

# Marketing Audience

When we are creating our marketing strategies it\'s important to know
what our target audience is. The following is a list of our current
target audiences for marketing materials:

-   Current Xubuntu users who want to show off Xubuntu pride through
    products
-   Users who wish to explore alternatives to Unity
-   Attendees at tech conferences who may already use Linux but want to
    learn more about Ubuntu and the flavors
-   Visitors to Ubuntu booths at who want other options than default

Note: See Strategy Document for target audience for Xubuntu itself (not
marketing).
