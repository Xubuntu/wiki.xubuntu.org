## Detailed specification

Areas of enhancing include, but are not limited to:
  - Community meetings
    - Scheduling? Chairs? Informal meetings?
    - Team reports!
  - Cooperation with the Ubuntu release team
    - Including communication with other teams on things affecting them/us
    - Prompt reporting!
  - Cooperation with Ubuntu Studio
    - Possibly some shared meetings?
    - Cooperation on updating offline documentation!
  - Generally, be in touch with other teams
    - Don't be afraid of the people, they're just humans
    - Communication on freeze exceptions etc. can be done by anybody in the community
  - Communication via Internet
    - Website and blog
    - Social media: Twitter, Google+
