## Detailed specification

***Note:* This specification is still in the drafting/discussion state. The decision will be made at a later date.**

This specification discusses the benefits and disadvantages of moving to a 1GB ISO size.

## Benefits
- Able to reintroduce Gnumeric
- Less maintaining work (considering we don't fill the ISO to the edge again...)
- Able to (re)introduce more languages

## Disadvantages
- Optical media limited to DVD
