### Design Proposals

Things to keep in mind when writing design proposals:

-   T-shirts in our budget are limited in colors (most vendors offer
    white and black, plus a few primary colors), so a design should
    expect to be placed on a plain background
-   If you propose a design that expects a dark background, keep in mind
    that for many vendors this means the design will print `even darker`
    than the image that is submitted
